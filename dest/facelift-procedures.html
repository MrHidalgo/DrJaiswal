WHAT IS A

Facelift?

The technical word for the operation is a rhytidectomy, however, most people refer to it as a facelift. The surgical procedure is created to rejuvenate the face. It involves lifting or tightening tissues in order to produce a smoother jawline, reduce jowls, increase the volume of the cheek, and/or improve the appearance of the neck.

Who are the Best Candidates for a Facelift?

There are many candidates for this revolutionary surgery. Below, we’ve included some of the patients we see on a day-to-day basis.

  • Patients who have undergone expected aging of the face are typically great candidates

  • Patients with jowls

  • Patients with loss of volume in the cheek

  • Patients with looseness of face and neck skin

  • Patients should be in good physical health and not smoke cigarettes

  • We request that our patients be psychologically stable and have realistic expectations of what the procedure can and cannot do

What Type of Facelift Does Dr. Jaiswal Perform?

Dr. Jaiswal has performed many types of facelifts including deep plane, high SMAS, and MACS. The differences are discussed during the consultation and the treatment recommendations are tailored to the individual patient.

Traditional

The deeper layer of tissue covering the muscle is released at various points and lifted. This is typically the most comprehensive facelift and restores cheek volume, jawline definition, neck contour, and reduces or eliminates jowling.

Mini facelift

A less invasive facelift method that typically acts only on improving the lower 1/3 of the face. It is used in patients who do not have advanced aging and are seeking an improvement of the jawline and/or jowls.

Understanding the Different Types of Facelifts

When deciding if you want to move forward with the operation, it’s first best to understand the different types of the procedure. While a traditional facelift is more popular, depending on the age of the patient and their desired results sometimes other facelift procedures can accomplish this. Below, we’ve included the different types of facial rejuvenation procedures we offer to our Las Vegas facelift patients.

Mid-facelift

The deeper layer of tissue covering the muscle is released at various points and lifted. This is typically the most comprehensive facelift and restores cheek volume, jawline definition, neck contour, and reduces or eliminates jowling.

liquid facelift

For select patients, using fillers throughout the face may produce improvement in cheek volume, fewer wrinkles, and a stronger jawline or chin. This is typically reserved for patients with only mild signs of aging.

What’s the First Step?

The first step is to schedule a consultation. During the consult, Dr. Jaiswal will assess your facial tissues and discuss your goals. Then he will make a recommendation of a treatment plan.

If you’re happy with Dr. Jaiswal’s recommendations surgery will be booked and we’ll provide pre and postoperative instructions. Please make sure to follow these instructions as closely as possible, as it will lead to optimal facelift results and reduce the chances of risk and complications.
